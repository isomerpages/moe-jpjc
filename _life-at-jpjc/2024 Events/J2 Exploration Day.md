<div align="justify">
	
<p>The 'J2 Exploration Day' took place on February 6, 2024, offering an enriching opportunity for J2 students to delve into new interests beyond the confines of the classroom. The primary objective of the event was to broaden students' horizons and foster a spirit of exploration and discovery.</p>
<p>
The main participants in this event were the J2 students themselves, eagerly exploring the various activities presented to them. The event featured a diverse range of activities organised by both school CCAs and external vendors, ensuring a rich and engaging experience for all attendees.</p>

<p>Highlights of the 'J2 Exploration Day' included a blend of sharing sessions facilitated by CCAs and vendor-led activities. From silk fan painting by the Chinese Cultural Society to learning instruments with the Chinese Orchestra and Concert Band, students had a chance to immerse themselves in different cultural and creative pursuits. Additionally, the Eco Club offered a terrarium workshop, while the Infocomm Club provided a Spike Prime course, catering to students' diverse interests and passions.</p>

<p>Moreover, external vendors added excitement to the event with activities such as archery, cup stacking, dart soccer, indoor darts, inline skating, kickboxing, and lasertag. Each student had the opportunity to participate in two activities throughout the session, ensuring a well-rounded experience for everyone involved.</p>

<p>Overall, the 'J2 Exploration Day' proved to be a resounding success, with students enjoying a fun and educational session while discovering new skills and interests along the way. It served as a testament to the school's commitment to holistic education and nurturing students' talents beyond academics.
</p>
