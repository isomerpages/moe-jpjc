<p>19 May was no ordinary day for #JPFam as we gathered early in the morning at Jurong Lake Gardens for our Road Run.  From start to finish, the energy of the Road Run was unmatched!🤩 
</p>
	
<p>The Road Run at Jurong Lake Gardens definitely brought the heat. Despite running under the hot sun, our #JPFam gave the race their all, but the vibes did not stop there — we were treated to amazing live performances of ‘Best Part’,  ‘Stay’ and ‘Stuck With You’ by our very own talented student performers who had the crowd singing along and soaking in the moment. JP Dance Club also displayed electrifying vibes with their performances to trendy K-Pop songs. 🎶 </p>
	
<p>It was all smiles on stage during the prize presentation segment where we awarded medals to the top runners of each student and staff category.</p>
	
<p>A huge congratulations to Taurus house 🐂 for clinching 1st place for this year’s road run! 🏆🏆  To mark the end of the event on a high note, we celebrated with the much anticipated ‘JP Atmosphere’ cheer📣📣 led by our newly appointed student council leaders and respective house captains.</p>
	
<p>From the track to the stage, it was all energy, teamwork, and unforgettable memories! 🏁✨We thank the teachers and students who played a big role in making this event a huge success!</p>
