The <strong>H1 and H2 Geography curriculum</strong> focuses on six geographical concepts which frame the syllabus content. The six geographical concepts are as follows:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Environment</li>
	<li>Place</li>
	<li>Process</li>
	<li>Scale</li>
	<li>Space and Time</li>
