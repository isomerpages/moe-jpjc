<h3><strong>Objectives of House</strong></h3>
<p>The House system, comprising Taurus, Aries, Ursa, Pavo, Aquila and Volans, enhances the school experience by fostering a vibrant culture that nurtures studentsâ€™ pride and sense of belonging to the college. Through opportunities to lead and serve in House activities throughout the year, House Captains and House Executives develop key leadership competencies, becoming confident influencers who rally their peers to participate actively in House events.</p>

<h3><strong>Programmes</strong></h3>
<p>Students enjoy a vibrant college experience through House activities such as Road Run, House Bonding Day, and Inter-House Games, led by their House Captains and House Executives. Through these events, students compete for House points in the bid for the coveted Champion House title.</p>

<p>All students have the opportunity to serve as House Executives, where they receive specialised leadership training. Through these experiences, they develop key leadership competencies, becoming confident influencers who rally their peers to participate actively in House events.</p>
